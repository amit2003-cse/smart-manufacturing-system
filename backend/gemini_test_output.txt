For the **Wire Drawing Machine**, safety is the top priority. Based on the Wire Manufacturing Knowledge Base, here are the safety rules and guidelines you must follow:

**Global Safety Guidelines:**
1.  **Stop Machine for Inspection:** Always stop the machine completely before performing any manual inspection.
2.  **No Operation with Faults:** Do not operate the machine if there are any known faults or technical issues.
3.  **Use Safety Equipment:** Always use the required personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety gear.
4.  **Follow Plant Procedures:** Strictly adhere to all established plant safety protocols and procedures.

**Machine-Specific Safety & Operational Precautions:**
5.  **Emergency Stop:** Ensure the emergency stop is functional. If the machine fails to start, check if the emergency stop is activated and reset it only when it is safe to do so.
6.  **Proper Setup:** Ensure the die is properly aligned and the lubrication system is functioning correctly before starting the process to prevent mechanical failure.
7.  **Monitor Parameters:** Continuously monitor wire diameter, tension, and speed to ensure the machine is operating within safe limits.

⚠️ **Note:** If you encounter a specific fault, always follow the Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures as per your plant's safety manual before attempting repairs.
